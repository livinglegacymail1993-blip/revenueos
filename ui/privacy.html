Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025. This policy describes how RevenueOS (“we”) handles information in connection with our service.

Information we do not collect

We do not ask for or store your Stripe password. We do not store Stripe API keys. Access to your Stripe data is granted via Stripe Connect OAuth only.

OAuth and session data

When you connect Stripe via Connect OAuth, we receive an access token from Stripe. In this MVP, OAuth tokens are stored in memory only (not on disk or in a database). They are not logged. We will move to encrypted, persistent storage in a future release and will update this policy accordingly.

Revocation

You can revoke RevenueOS’s access to your Stripe account at any time from your Stripe Dashboard (Connect → Account connections). Revoking access invalidates our token; we will no longer be able to read your Stripe data.

Data we process

We use Stripe data (e.g. subscriptions) only to compute metrics and generate your operator report. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing. We use minimal, read-only scopes where possible.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the operator of this deployment.
