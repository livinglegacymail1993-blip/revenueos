Security

RevenueOS is designed with a trust-first, minimal-access approach for financial data.

We do not store API keys. OAuth tokens are stored in memory only in this MVP and will move to encrypted storage in a future release.

Stripe access

Access to your Stripe account is granted only via Stripe Connect OAuth. You authorize our application once; Stripe issues a time-limited token. We never see or store your Stripe login credentials.

Revocation

You can revoke RevenueOS’s access at any time from your Stripe Dashboard: go to Connect → Account connections and remove RevenueOS. This immediately invalidates our token; we cannot access your data after that.

Minimal scopes

We request read-only access where possible. We do not perform write operations (charges, refunds, etc.) on your Stripe account. We use the minimum scopes required to compute subscription-based metrics and generate your operator report.

Session cookies

We use a signed session cookie to associate your browser with your connected Stripe token. The cookie is HTTP-only and SameSite. We do not store tokens in local storage or in files.

No database (MVP)

In this MVP we do not use a database. Session and token data exist only in process memory and are lost on restart. This avoids persistence of sensitive data until we implement encrypted storage.
