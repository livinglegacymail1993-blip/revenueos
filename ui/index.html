Security FAQ

How do you access my data? We use Stripe Connect OAuth. You authorize our app once; Stripe gives us a time-limited token. We never see or store your Stripe login.

Can I revoke access? Yes. In your Stripe Dashboard → Connect → Account connections, you can revoke RevenueOS at any time.

What do you store? In this MVP we store OAuth tokens in memory only (not on disk). They are not logged. We will move to encrypted storage in a future release.
